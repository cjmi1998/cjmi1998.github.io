About

Carina Imbornone is a writer born in Massachusetts. She lives and works in New York, NY. She received a BA and MA in English from Boston University in 2020.

She is the Business Manager for The Drift and a Fiction Reader for The New England Review. She has worked at Farrar, Straus and Giroux, Highsnobiety, and Interview, where she edited Talk Hole.
